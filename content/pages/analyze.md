# SEO Content Analyzer - Audit Your Content for Better Rankings

ContentRankLabs' SEO analyzer helps you identify and fix issues that could be hurting your search engine rankings. Get detailed insights into your content's performance and optimization opportunities.

## How the SEO Analyzer Works

Our advanced AI-powered analyzer evaluates your content across multiple SEO factors:

### On-Page SEO Analysis
- **Title Tag Optimization**: Length, keyword usage, and uniqueness
- **Meta Description**: Character count and keyword inclusion
- **Heading Structure**: H1, H2, H3 hierarchy and optimization
- **Keyword Density**: Optimal keyword distribution
- **Content Length**: Minimum word count recommendations

### Technical SEO Checks
- **Mobile Friendliness**: Responsive design validation
- **Page Speed**: Loading time analysis
- **Schema Markup**: Structured data implementation
- **Internal Linking**: Link structure and anchor text
- **Image Optimization**: Alt tags and file sizes

### Content Quality Metrics
- **Readability Score**: Flesch-Kincaid and other readability tests
- **Engagement Factors**: Content depth and user experience
- **Duplicate Content**: Originality and uniqueness checks
- **Grammar and Spelling**: Writing quality assessment

## SEO Scoring System

Get a comprehensive SEO score from 0-100 based on:
- **Technical SEO (30%)**: Site speed, mobile optimization, schema
- **On-Page SEO (40%)**: Keywords, meta tags, content structure
- **Content Quality (20%)**: Readability, engagement, uniqueness
- **Off-Page SEO (10%)**: Backlinks and social signals

## Actionable Recommendations

Receive specific, actionable recommendations to improve your SEO score:
- **High Priority**: Critical issues affecting rankings
- **Medium Priority**: Important optimizations
- **Low Priority**: Nice-to-have improvements
