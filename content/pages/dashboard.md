# ContentRankLabs Dashboard - Your Content Creation Hub

Welcome to your ContentRankLabs dashboard, where you can generate, analyze, and optimize your SEO content with ease.

## Generate New Content

Use our AI-powered content generator to create high-quality articles, blog posts, and marketing materials. Simply enter your topic, target keywords, and let Claude AI do the rest.

### Content Generation Features:
- **Topic Research**: AI analyzes trending topics and search intent
- **Keyword Optimization**: Natural keyword integration for better rankings
- **Structure Optimization**: Proper heading hierarchy and content flow
- **Schema Integration**: Automatic FAQ and how-to schema markup

## Analyze Existing Content

Upload or paste your existing content to get a comprehensive SEO analysis including:
- **SEO Score**: Overall optimization rating
- **Keyword Analysis**: Density, prominence, and competition
- **Readability Score**: Flesch-Kincaid and other metrics
- **Technical Issues**: Missing meta tags, broken links, etc.

## Content History

Access your previously generated content and analyses. Track your progress and refine your SEO strategy over time.

## API Integration

Connect ContentRankLabs to your CMS, blog platform, or content management system for seamless content creation workflows.

## Best Practices for SEO Content

1. **Keyword Research**: Use tools like Google Keyword Planner
2. **Content Structure**: Include H1, H2, H3 headings
3. **Internal Linking**: Link to related content on your site
4. **Mobile Optimization**: Ensure content works on all devices
5. **Regular Updates**: Keep content fresh and up-to-date
